Lesson 2 Part 3: Numbers 20 - 100

Numbers 20-100 are much like English, where each multiple of ten has a name, and these are combined with the numbers being organised from highest to lowest (i.e. one hundred twenty one = сто двадцать один, stress patterns the same), just like in English. They are usually in the form [number]дцать or [number]десят ([number]-ten, i.e. ten of [number]), with the exception of 40 (which is СОрок), 90 (which is девяНОСто), and 100 (which is сто).
