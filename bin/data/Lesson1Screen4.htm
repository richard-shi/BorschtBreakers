Lesson 1 Part 4: Modifying letters

 

There are two modifying letters in Russian. They have no sound of their own, but modify the sound of a previous consonant, and thus cannot come at the beginning of a word.

Letter

Name

Function

Ьь

Магкий знак (M’AGH-k’iy znak) Soft sign

Softens the preceding consonant. Vowels that follow it maintain their ‘y’ sound as if they were preceded by another vowel (only soft vowels can follow a soft sign). Sometimes a soft sign follows an unsoftenable consonant to indicate gender. The unsoftenable consonant does not change.

Ъъ

Твёрдый знак (TV’OR-diy znak) Hard sign

Makes a consonant hard. Vowels that follow it maintain their ‘y’ sound as if they were preceded by another vowel (only soft vowels can follow a hard sign).
