Multiples of 1000 (like ten thousand) are referred to with a number before it and a dropping of the final а. For example, ten thousand = десять тысяч. For multiples of a thousand with a ones digit less than 5, use тысячи, i.e. три тысячи. This pattern holds until 999 thousand.
